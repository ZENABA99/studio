# **App Name**: AloeVeraShop

## Core Features:

- Product Catalog: Display a catalog of Aloe Vera-based products with descriptions, prices, and images.
- User Authentication: Allow users and distributors to create accounts, log in, and manage their profiles.
- Shopping Cart: Enable users to add products to a shopping cart, view the cart contents, and proceed to checkout.
- Order Management: Process orders, manage payments through Stripe or PayPal, and update order status.
- Automated Email: Automated email sending for confirming orders, sending invoices, password reset, etc.
- Distributor Management: Manage distributor accounts, commissions, and product distribution.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Light, natural green (#A7D1AB) to reflect the Aloe Vera plant and natural ingredients.
- Background color: Off-white (#F9F6F2), a very lightly tinted desaturated yellow, to provide a clean and calming backdrop.
- Accent color: Warm gold (#E4B96E), an analogous color, to highlight important elements and calls to action.
- Body font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif font, providing a modern and neutral look suitable for product descriptions and general content.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk', a proportional sans-serif font, to convey a contemporary and accessible feel.
- Use clean, line-based icons representing product categories, benefits, and features.
- Employ a clean, grid-based layout with ample white space to enhance readability and focus on product presentation.
